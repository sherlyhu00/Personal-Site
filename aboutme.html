Introduction

Hello! I'm Yirui (Shirley) Hu, a GIS Analyst and Spatial Data Specialist with a passion for turning complex geographic data into meaningful insights. Currently pursuing dual Master's degrees in Geographic Information Science and Technology and City & Regional Planning at Georgia Institute of Technology.

My work centers on building geospatial models, spatial databases, and data-driven workflows to solve real-world problems — from siting infrastructure and assessing community accessibility to visualizing environmental and demographic patterns across urban landscapes.

With expertise in ArcGIS Pro, Python (GeoPandas, ArcPy, OSMnx), SQL, and R, I design and implement end-to-end GIS solutions — from raw data processing and spatial analysis to interactive dashboards and StoryMaps that communicate findings clearly to diverse audiences.

Career Goals

My career goal is to apply advanced GIS and spatial data skills to solve complex, real-world problems — delivering actionable insights through rigorous analysis and compelling visualization.

Building Geospatial Solutions

I aim to develop robust GIS workflows and spatial databases that support data-driven decision-making across sectors — including urban planning, infrastructure, environmental management, and public health. My focus is on building scalable, reproducible pipelines from raw data ingestion through final map outputs.

Advancing Spatial Data Analysis

I'm committed to applying cutting-edge spatial analysis methods — including network analysis, multi-criteria suitability modeling, and geostatistical techniques — to quantify accessibility, equity, and environmental patterns. I aim to continually deepen my proficiency in Python-based geospatial libraries and open-source GIS tools.

Creating Impactful Visualizations

Effective GIS work requires communicating findings to non-technical audiences. I aspire to create interactive dashboards, StoryMaps, and data products that make spatial analysis accessible and actionable for stakeholders, planners, and community members alike.

Integrating Emerging Technologies

I am excited to explore the intersection of GIS with machine learning, remote sensing, and big data platforms to tackle increasingly complex spatial challenges. From predictive site selection models to real-time spatial analytics, I aim to push the boundaries of what geospatial analysis can achieve.

Through these goals, I hope to grow as a GIS professional who bridges technical depth with practical impact — helping organizations and communities make better, spatially informed decisions.
