Introduction

Hello! I'm Yirui (Shirley) Hu, a Transportation Planner and GIS Specialist with a passion for creating more accessible and sustainable transportation systems. Currently pursuing dual Master's degrees in City & Regional Planning and Geographic Information Science and Technology at Georgia Institute of Technology.

My work focuses on leveraging spatial analysis and data-driven approaches to address transportation challenges, particularly in the areas of accessibility planning for vulnerable populations, public transit optimization, and sustainable mobility solutions.

With a strong foundation in both urban planning and economics, combined with advanced technical skills in GIS and programming, I strive to bridge the gap between technical analysis and practical transportation planning solutions that serve diverse communities.

Career Goals

My career goal is to contribute to the development of more equitable, accessible, and sustainable transportation systems that serve all members of our communities.

Advancing Transportation Accessibility

I aim to specialize in accessibility planning for vulnerable populations, including wheelchair users, elderly residents, and low-income communities. By integrating advanced GIS analysis with community engagement, I seek to identify and address transportation gaps that create barriers to mobility and opportunity.

Promoting Sustainable Mobility

I'm committed to advancing sustainable transportation solutions, including public transit optimization, micromobility programs, and electric vehicle infrastructure planning. My goal is to help communities transition toward low-carbon transportation systems while ensuring these solutions remain accessible and affordable.

Building Community Partnerships

Effective transportation planning requires meaningful community engagement. I aspire to work collaboratively with local governments, transit agencies, community organizations, and residents to develop transportation plans that reflect community needs and priorities. Grant writing and funding acquisition will be key tools in bringing these visions to life.

Leveraging Data-Driven Innovation

As transportation systems become increasingly complex, I aim to leverage emerging technologies and data analytics to inform planning decisions. From real-time transit data analysis to simulation modeling, I'm excited to apply cutting-edge methods to solve real-world transportation challenges.

Through these goals, I hope to make a lasting impact on how people move through their communities, ensuring that transportation systems enhance quality of life for all.
